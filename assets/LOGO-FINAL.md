## ✅ Locked In: Monogram-Only Design

**Design Decision:** CL monogram without shield - luxury brand aesthetic, timeless, unique

## Final Logo Specifications

### Visual Elements

- **Primary Mark:** Intertwined "CL" monogram
- **Color:** Gold Leaf (#D4AF37) on White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent:** Small obsidian dot (#1D1D1F) above monogram (wax seal)
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, luxury typography
- **NO shield, NO frame, NO border**

### Why This Works

✅ **Unique** - Not competing with 10,000+ shield logos  
✅ **Premium** - Luxury brand aesthetic (Chanel, Louis Vuitton style)  
✅ **Timeless** - Will look good in 50 years  
✅ **Scalable** - Recognizable from 16px to billboard size  
✅ **Confident** - Doesn't hide behind protective symbols  
✅ **Relevant** - Focus on legacy and sovereignty, not security

## Usage Guidelines

### Primary Use

- App icon on mobile devices
- Favicon for website
- Logo mark on landing page (navigation)
- Email signatures
- Marketing materials

### Color Variants

- **Primary:** Gold (#D4AF37) on White (#FFFFFF)
- **Reverse:** White on Obsidian Ink (#1D1D1F) for dark backgrounds
- **Monochrome:** Black or single color when needed

### DO NOT

- Add shields, frames, or containers
- Change the monogram typography
- Remove the wax seal dot accent
- Use gradients or 3D effects
